Greatest Streaks

E-Sports:

Ninjas in Pyjamas
Ninjas in pyjamas is a Counter Strike Global Offensive team from sweden that managed to win 87 straight maps on LAN without losing a single one. It's a record that has never been replicated in Counter Strike Global Offensive, and remains one of the most impressive E-sports records of all time.

Gonzalo Barrios
Gonzalo Barrios, known by his gamertag ZeRo, is a professional Super Smash Bros player. He is considered the best Super Smash Bros for Wii U player in the world. In 2016, ZeRo was featured in the Guinness Book of World Records Gamer's Edition for his 56 consecutive Smash tournament victories.

Choi Yeon Sung
Choi Yeon-sung, also known as iloveoov, is a retired professional Korean StarCraft player. iloveoov played the role of a terran and had an impeccable 27 win streak in Starcraft and his streaks versus Zerg are particularly notable.

Baseball:

New York Giants
The San Francisco Giants of Major League Baseball originated in New York City as the New York Gothams in 1883 and were known as the New York Giants from 1885. They managed to win 26 consecutive games from September 7, 1916 till September 30, 1916. This is considered to be the greatest win streak in baseball.

Cleveland Indians
The Cleveland Indians are an American professional baseball team based in Cleveland, Ohio. They compete in Major League Baseball (MLB) as a member club of the American League. They managed a 22 consecutive win streak

Chicago Cubs
The Chicago Cubs are an American professional baseball team based in Chicago, Illinois. The Cubs compete in Major League Baseball (MLB) as a member club of the National League (NL) Central division. The Cubs are known as "the North Siders" and have managed to win 21 consecutive wins from September 4, 1935 till September 28, 1935.

Golfing:

Byron Nelson
John Byron Nelson Jr was a professional American golfer between 1935 and 1946, widely considered one of the greatest golfers of all time. In 1945, Byron Nelson won an amazing 11 PGA Tour tournaments in a row that he played. He won 18 tournaments overall that year! It is certainly the most remarkable year any golfer has ever had on any tour, at any time.

Tiger Woods
Eldrick Tont "Tiger" Woods is an American professional golfer. He is tied for first in PGA Tour wins and ranks second in men's major championship and also holds numerous golf records. Tiger Woods won an amazing 7 PGA Championship and British Open tournaments in a row that he played.

Hockey:

Pittsburgh Penguins
During the 1992-1993 Season of the NHL. The Pittsburgh Penguins managed to win up to 17 consecutive wins without a single loss. The streak began in 03/09/1993 and ended in 04/10/1993. This is considered to be the greatest win streak in the NHL.

Columbus Blue Jackets
During the 2016-2017 Season of the NHL. The Columbus Blue Jackets managed to win up to 16 consecutive wins without a single loss. The streak began in 11/29/2016 and ended in 01/03/2017. This is considered to be the second greatest win streak in the NHL.

Jahangir Khan
Pakistan squash legend Jahangir Khan is famed for winning 555 matches in a row. Rod Gilmour and Alan Thatcher say nobody was keeping proper records during Khan's five-and-a-half-year unbeaten run from 1981 to 1986. However, Khan insisted the figure could be even higher than 555, as it doesn't take into account exhibition matches. His winning streak from 1981 was finally brought to a halt by New Zealand's Ross Norman at the 1986 world championships in Toulouse.
