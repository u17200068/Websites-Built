About the Event

This Event will take place on May 11, 2020 and is a tournament hosted by StarLadder. Counter Strike Global Offensive is a competitive 5 v 5 First-Person Shooter game that was release in 2012. The tournament is a major one where 8 Teams will compete for a 1 million dollar prize pool with 1st place receiving $500,000, 2nd place receiving $150,000, 3rd and 4th place receiving $70,000 and 5th-8th place receiving $35,000 each. The Quarterfinals and Semifinals consist of a BO3.
