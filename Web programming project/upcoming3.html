About the Event

This Event will take place on December 29th 2019 in the State Farm Arena in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. The event will be covered by Showtime and contains two of the best athletes sponsored by Mayweather Promotions. Two-time super featherweight champion Gervonta Davis moves up to take on former unified world champion Yuriorkis Gamboa for the lightweight title. In the co-feature, Jean Pascal defends his light heavyweight belt against two-division champ Badou Jack.
