About the Event

This Event will take place on December 22nd 2019 in the Toyota Arena in Ontario, California. The event will be covered by FOX. Tony Harrison and Jermell Charlo will be competing for the WBC Super Welterweight Title, Rigondeaux will move down to bantamweight for the first time in his career to face Liborio Solis for a vacant WBA World Bantamweight Championship.
