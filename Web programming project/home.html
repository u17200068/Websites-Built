Welcome to Undefeated

In here you will not be able to find every achievement, rather this is a subjective showcase of the achievements accomplished by various athletes that I have found the most impressive. The list will not contain only undefeated records but also undefeated streaks that have yet to be broken. I will also be including E-sports as a sport and will include player records and streaks as well as team records and streaks. You will also be able to find information on most of the athletes and various events, when and where they will be taking place as well as the athletes or teams that will be competing. The main focus will be primarily on combat sports but I will also try to present athletes/teams from various other sports.
